Product details

CeraVe Hydrating Cleanser lotion gently removes dirt and oil while increasing skin hydration after just one use. It sweeps away dirt, excess oil and impurities without stripping away moisture, the cleanser uses Hyaluronic Acid to strengthen and restore the natural barrier function for long-lasting hydration. Time-release technology ensures skin stays light, clear and refreshed throughout the day and night.

Key features of CeraVe Hydrating Cleanser

  • Suitable for normal to dry skin
  • Cleanses and hydrates
  • Fragrance free
  • With 3 essential ceramides to protect skin's natural barrier
  • Soap free
  • Non-comedogenic

Usage / Instructions

Use morning and evening. Apply to wet hands and massage into the skin, then rinse thoroughly.
